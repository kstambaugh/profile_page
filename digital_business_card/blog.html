Hey there, and welcome to "my BLOG".

My goal here is to share with you my journey of discovering who I am and by doing so, help you to discover something about yourself.

At the young young age of 33, I came to find out that I had ADHD and that everything I thought I knew about the condition, was absolutely inncorrect. As I learned more, I realized that it explained away A LOT of my behaviors over the years, especially the "hobbie jumping". In fact, in just the last 5 years (it's currently May 2023) I have picked up reading, piano, guitar, stained glass, woodworking, woodturning (yes, its different), gardening, painting, drawing, 3d printing, table top role playing games, and programming. Now, I've maintained a legitimate passion for most of these but it's hard to balance so many hobbies, especially working full time, school part-time, being a husband, a father of 3; life gets in the way of enjoying life.

Now, here I am, using something I'm very passionate about (web design) to build the medium to discuss my other passions. Here we go!
